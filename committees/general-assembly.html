General Assembly

Disarmament and International Security Committee

Nausherwan Abbasi

Chair DISEC

Assalamulaikum,

This isn't going to be your typical committee. You're going to have to be dynamic, influential, and above all, innovative. The criteria for excellence in this committee isn't going to be your solely your research, or your speeches, or simply the quality of your written material. It will be the overall impact you have on the people around you, the extent to which you can persuade others to accept your point of view, the power and impact of your speeches, especially political rhetoric(because I've always found a barrage of statistics and facts a good way to put myself to sleep). We'll be looking for all of these things while deciding that elusive best delegate award. That said, best of luck come September.

Regards,
Nausherwan Abbasi
