Payment Method

Option 1: Payment in Cash:-

Delegations can come to Aitchison College, Lahore, Pakistan, and kindly submit in the payment in cash to the Accounts Office, Administration Block. Please use the Sunder Das Gate for this purpose. An official receipt will be provided in return as proof of payment. Local teams are especially encouraged to opt for this process.

Option 2: Payment through Bank Draft or Money Order

Delegations may send through TCS, a Bank Draft or Pay Order in the name of the Principal Aitchison College, to the accounts office as a means of payment. Please ensure that the envelope has the Accounts Department mentioned in the address, and please mention your respective school’s/ delegation’s name very clearly as well. All outstation teams may opt for this means of payment. Aitchison College’s official address for the TCS delivery is as follows:

Aitchison College
Shahrah-e-Quaid-e-Azam,
Lahore 54000, Pakistan

Option 3: Payment on the spot:- (Only For International Delegations)

All International Delegations may pay on the day of the conference i.e 11th of September, during the registrations process. Delegations are advised to ensure that there is no discrepancy between the amount payable, and the cash they have available on the spot. All International Delegations are required to submit their payment in US Dollars.

*Please note that the deadline for the payment is the 10th of August 2014. Any delegation that is unable to meet this deadline will not be allowed to participate at ACMUN VI. If there is any delegation, which wants an extension to the given deadline or request an alternative mode of payment, should contact the Director Registrations ( Mustafa Amjad: + 92 333 4562850 | Ali Hassan Raza: + 92 333 5000202 ) before hand. If the ACMUN VI team feels the need to extend the given the deadline on a later basis, we shall do so, but the given deadline should be strictly followed.
