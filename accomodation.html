Accomodation Details

We here, at the Aitchison College Model United Nations Society want to ensure that our distinguished delegates not only participate in a competitive MUN conference with heated debate, but also that they have a very comfortable stay in one of the most beautiful cities of Pakistan! Following are the three accommodation plans we have to offer:

Bahawalpur House (International Teams Only)

Bahawalpur House

A five star hotel located on Mall Road, Lahore, a mere five minutes drive from the College, Hotel One embodies the concept of luxury and affordability. Complimentary breakfast is offered, along with facilities such as access to a swimming pool, laundry service, restaurants with a multitude of cuisines, and round the clock Wi-Fi Internet, and air conditioned rooms. Room rates are as follows:

- Single occupancy room: PKR 7520 per night (inclusive of taxes) i.e. USD 76 per night (approx.)
- Double occupancy room: PKR 8800 per night (inclusive of taxes) i.e. USD 88 per night (approx.)
- Extra Mattress Charges: PKR 1252 (inclusive of taxes) i.e. USD 13 (approx.)

For further information, please visit: http://www.hotelone.com.pk

Hotel Crown Plaza Lahore

Hotel Crown Plaza

Another hotel located less than 2 kilometers from the College; this is a 4 star luxury hotel with comfortable air-conditioned rooms. Breakfasts, access to swimming pools, and health club will be provided free of cost to hotel customers. Crown Plaza also has many restaurants offering Pakistani as well as other cuisines. Room rates are as follows:

- Single occupancy room: PKR 8400 per night (inclusive of taxes) i.e. USD 84 per night (approx.)
- Double occupancy room: PKR 10000 per night (inclusive of taxes) i.e. USD 100 per night (approx.)
- Extra Mattress Charges: PKR 2000 (inclusive of taxes) i.e. USD 20 (approx.)

For further information, please visit: http://www.crownplazalahore.com



*Please note that all rates may be subject to change at the discretion of the respective hotel’s management, and prices in USD may change due to exchange rate fluctuations.
* Aitchison College will provide free pick and drop service from the conference venue to the respective hotels of the delegations.
