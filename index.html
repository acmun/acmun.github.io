About Us

The ACMUN society ever since its inception has continued to flourish and has become one of the most widely known and successful MUN societies of Pakistan.

Here we are, at the sixth edition of the Aitchison College Model United Nations Conference, ACMUN VII. We have tried to make it more beneficial and have assimilated a distinct set of committees for an improved level and standard of intellectual debate. The event is spread over the course of 4 days, with committee sessions and much more. Do not miss your chance to become a part of the ACMUN VI team.
