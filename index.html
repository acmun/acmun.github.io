What we are

Know more about ACMUN

The ACMUN society ever since its inception has continued to flourish and has become one of the most widely known and successful MUN societies of Pakistan. The society as a whole, has not only had regional and national successes but our MUNers have won numerous Best Delegations at significant International MUN’s which include the likes of Harvard MUN, Berkeley International Model United Nations, Asian international MUN, Cathedral MUN and Modern School International Model United Nations. The members of the society who serve as your chairs have helped in establishing the society as one of the most prestigious MUN society of Pakistan. Our MUNers have strived for what they are today; using their skills and experience, they will be a good experience for all of us.

The platform for the first ever ACMUN conference was laid by Haider Imran Zali in 2009 and the conference in itself, was a huge achievement for the entire College. Ever since the breakthrough was provided, the ACMUN society has been surpassing all limits of success. Continuing the work of their predecessors, the ACMUN society was handed over into the hands of the versatile duo of Abeer Mustafa and Faraz Ayaz and this marked the 2nd edition of the conference. The charge was then taken over by Kehaan Manjee and Amaan Naseer who continued to improve the society and took the society to it’s peak. The 4th edition was presented by the collaborative efforts of the President of the ACMUN Society Hamza Bajwa and the then Head boy Hammad Aslam. The 5th edition was run by Hassan Ayub, Abdullah Mannan and Daanish Izhar. Last year’s conference, ACMUN VII was by far the most successful of all conferences headed by the then-president Muhammad Ahmed Tajammul.

Here we are, at the seventh edition of the Aitchison College Model United Nations Conference, “ACMUN VII”. We have tried to make it more beneficial and have assimilated a distinct set of committees for an improved level and standard of intellectual debate. The event is spread over the course of 4 days, with committee sessions and much more. Do not miss your chance to become a part of the ACMUN VII family.

Looking forward to meeting you all in February!
ACMUN VII Team
