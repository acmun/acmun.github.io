Committee Topic

Topic : Syrian Rehabilitation

A brutal affair in the war torn Syrian Arab Republic, The Cabinet of Ministers under the Syrian regime Bashar Al Asad faces some of the biggest challenges not only in the history of the country but the history of the world. Since 2011 Syria has been engulfed in a civil war, with a plethora of fighting parties such as “The Free Syrian Army” rejecting the rule of Damascus and waging a war against the government and “The Islamic State of Iraq or Syria” or “Daesh” as it is locally known fighting and controlling the region from “Abu Kamal” to “Maskanah”. With the war still persisting and the fighting groups not laying down their weapons to the Syrian Government an the infrastructure of the country damaged, a major problem still persists for the rule. The Ministers under Bashar Al Asad have a plenty of things to sort out, from taking steps that result in an end to the current geopolitical crisis prevailing in the country to adopting certain policies and securing deals that will benefit the overall progress of the currently much affected Syria. Delegates will serve the role of Syrian Ministers under Bashar Al Asad, taking steps under their portfolio powers that re-establish peace once again and contributes to the smooth rehabilitation of the Syrian Arab Republic. Know this, however: The President of Syria will see his beloved country to greatness, with the security of his regime. *Bashar Al Asad's Council of Ministers is a continual crisis committee
